Soup Recipe

Description

This is a comforting soup recipe that is perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup pasta (optional)
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion, carrots, and celery. Saut√© until the vegetables are softened, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.
  4. Pour in the broth and add the diced tomatoes.
  5. Stir in the dried thyme, salt, and pepper.
  6. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 20 minutes.
  7. If using pasta, add it to the soup and cook according to package instructions until al dente.
  8. Taste and adjust seasoning if necessary.
  9. Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley if desired.
