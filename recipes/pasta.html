Pasta Recipe

Description

This is a simple pasta recipe that anyone can make at home.

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta of your choice
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil water in a large pot and cook the pasta according to package instructions.
  2. In a separate pan, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Add minced garlic and saut√© until fragrant.
  4. Add the diced tomatoes and cook for about 5 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Drain the pasta and add it to the sauce, tossing to combine.
  7. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil if desired.
